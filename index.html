Hi! I'm Will, and I'm in my 2A term, studying Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I like coding, tinkering, and playing Settlers of Catan, and this is my personal website.

I love making things. I love the idea of it, and I love doing it. I'm studying Mechatronics Engineering for exactly that reason: I want to be able to build advanced robotic and technical devices with a good understanding of the whole process.

To that end I built this website, and a variety of other projects, including an iOS game and an online bot which trades in Bitcoin (details and videos of which are available at this website's "Projects" page). I've used Autocad and SolidWorks, web servers and Arduinos, and more or less everything else I've ever needed to get the job done. I'm also part of a student design team, my class representative for the university's Engineering Society, a good writer, and a comfortable public speaker.
